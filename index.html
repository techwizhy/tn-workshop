OUR MISSION

About the Workshop

The Tamil Nadu Youth Political Workshop is a dedicated non-partisan initiative aimed at transforming passionate young citizens into active public leaders. By demystifying the operations of democracy and providing concrete training, we bridge the gap between community needs and systemic governance.

Governance Literacy

Explore the architecture of power from the Gram Panchayat to the State Secretariat.

Policy Formulation

Develop research, drafting, and analysis skills to design effective public policies.

Public Advocacy

Learn community mobilization, coalition building, and data-driven communication.

GENDER INCLUSION

Fostering Female Leadership

Tamil Nadu has been a pioneer in legislative gender representation, reserving 50% of local body seats for women. However, effective political power requires specialized training, strategic mentoring, and robust networks. Our curriculum focuses on empowering young women to assume decision-making roles in panchayats, municipalities, and policy research institutions.

QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to participate in the workshops?

Citizens residing in Tamil Nadu between the ages of 18 and 35 are eligible. We encourage applications from students, community organizers, village panchayat candidates, and local advocates.

Is there an entry or registration fee for the workshop?

No, the core workshop is completely free of charge. Selected participants will receive instructional guides, lunch, and certificate resources. Optional donations can be made to support the program.

What is the selection process for the program?

Since seats are limited to 40 per batch, candidates are selected based on their registration application form responses, leadership interest, and active civic involvement.

Is this workshop affiliated with any political party?

No, the workshop is strictly non-partisan. We focus on procedural systems, local governance rules, campaign mechanisms, and policy writing frameworks, independent of political ideologies.

Are the sessions conducted in Tamil or English?

Sessions are bilingual. The slides and reading materials are in English, while the lectures and case study debates are explained in both Tamil and English to ensure maximum accessibility.

What does the Civic Action Project involve?

The project requires participants to identify an issue in their local neighbourhood or ward, research the governing rules, write a policy brief or action plan, and submit it to the local authority.

Do you offer travel or accommodation reimbursement?

We do not provide accommodation. However, local travel allowances may be arranged for selected participants from marginalized backgrounds. Please request this during registration.

Will I receive a certificate after completion?

Yes, all participants who attend the entire scheduled module and submit their Civic Action Project will receive a verified Certificate of Civic Leadership.

How can I join the alumni mentorship network?

Once you graduate and receive your certificate, you will automatically receive an invitation link to the alumni portal and local district channels for ongoing mentorship.

Are there virtual or online workshops available?

Yes, the Digital Advocacy and Communication module is conducted online. All other modules are in-person to facilitate networking and interactive workshops.
